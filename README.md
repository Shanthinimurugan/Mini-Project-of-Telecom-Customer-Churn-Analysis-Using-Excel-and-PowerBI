# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis using Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer attrition remains a significant concern for telecom industry. This project analyzes customer churn by exploring customer demographics, service subscriptions, contract details, billing information, and customer feedback to identify the key factors influencing churn.

Using **Excel** for data preprocessing and **Power BI** for data modeling, visualization, and interactive dashboards, the project provides actionable insights that help improve customer retention and business performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A telecom company is experiencing a high customer churn rate, resulting in reduced revenue and increased customer acquisition costs. The company needs to understand why customers leave and identify high-risk customer segments to implement effective retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze customer churn trends and patterns.
- Identify customer segments with the highest churn.
- Examine the impact of contract type, tenure, age, referrals, and marketing offers.
- Evaluate the influence of internet services, phone services, and value-added services on churn.
- Analyze customer churn across different cities and population categories.
- Predict customers who are at high risk of churn.
- Recommend business strategies to improve customer retention.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Approximately **26.56%** of customers have churned.
- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** exhibit the highest churn.
- **New customers** are more likely to leave than long-term customers.
- **Senior Citizens** experience the highest churn among age groups.
- **Fiber Optic** customers have significantly higher churn compared to DSL and Cable users.
- Customers with **high monthly charges**, **no referrals**, and **no marketing offers** are at greater risk of churn.
- Customers without value-added services such as **Online Security**, **Online Backup**, **Premium Tech Support**, and **Device Protection** churn more frequently.
- Approximately **803 customers** are identified as high-risk for future churn.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch to One-Year or Two-Year contracts.
- Improve Fiber Optic service quality.
- Offer personalized discounts before contract renewal.
- Strengthen referral and loyalty programs.
- Promote value-added services.
- Reduce churn in low-population regions while targeting medium- and high-population areas with retention campaigns.
